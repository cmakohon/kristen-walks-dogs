About Me

Hey neighbors! I’m Kristen and I moved to South End in June 2017 with my husband and my puppy, Hobbes. I've really enjoyed getting to know my neighbors and their dogs at the bark park! I have owned and worked with dogs since I was old enough to walk to my neighbor’s house. I have experience with large and small breeds and I love them all! I have a degree in Biology from UNC Chapel Hill and am currently in the Occupational Therapy Assistant program at CPCC. My classes are online at the moment, so I have lots of flexibility in my schedule to walk dogs. I look forward to meeting you soon!

- kristen
